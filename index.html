How AniMottos works

We help kids learn about their strengths through short games and stories. Each child gets an animal, a short motto, and a small mission to try each week.

  1. Play a fun quiz (stories, drag & drop, pick-a-path)
  2. Meet your AniMotto and motto card
  3. Try weekly missions & collect badges
